Policy Sections

POL-01

ChatGPT & AI Tool Use

AI tools may be used only when explicitly allowed for the assignment. Students must disclose any AI assistance and remain fully responsible for submitted work.

  • Disclose prompts and outputs when required.
  • No AI use during closed-book tests unless announced.
  • Plagiarism rules apply to AI-generated text/code.
POL-02

Tests & Exams

Tests assess individual understanding. Attendance is required unless excused in advance with documentation. Makeup assessments follow course rules.

  • Bring required identification for all exams.
  • Late arrival may reduce available exam time.
  • No unauthorized devices during testing.
POL-03

Assignments & Submission

Submit assignments by the listed deadline. Late penalties and extension requests are handled according to syllabus policy and fairness standards.

  • Follow file naming and format requirements.
  • Group work must include contribution statements.
  • Regrade requests must be submitted within one week.
